About Us

Esubu Tech Academy is dedicated to empowering children with essential tech and cognitive skills. Our online holiday camp offers fun, interactive classes in Scratch programming and Chess, designed for kids aged 6–16. All sessions are conducted via Zoom by experienced instructors.
